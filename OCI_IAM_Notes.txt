IAM:
Identity access management
AuthN -- authentication -- who are you ?
AuthZ -- authorization -- what are your privileges ?

Tenancy --> Identity Domain --> Users --> Groups --> Policies --> Compartments --> Resource

Identity Domain:
Region specific intial authentication validator-- Password and MFA

Compartment:
Logical grouping of resources
eg: All network resources in a network compartment
Resource from different compartments can interact with each other.
Resources can also be moved to other compartments.
Resources from multiple regions can be in same compartment
Quotas and budgets on compartments.
Quotas--resource limit
Budget--financial limit

Sample Identity domain: Sandbox
Sample user: OCIADMIN
Sample group: oci-admin-group
Sample Policy: oci-admin-group
Policy Statements
Allow group 'sandbox-domain'/'oci-admin-group' to manage buckets in compartment sandbox
Allow group 'sandbox-domain'/'oci-admin-group' to manage objects in compartment sandbox
